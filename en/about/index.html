About CS Kites Hong Kong

Our Story

CS Kites was founded by the Mak Family and has been flying high for nearly 40 years. Founder Benny Mak and his family share a deep passion for kite culture, dedicated to bringing quality kites and joyful experiences to families across Hong Kong.

From a small shop to one of Hong Kong's most trusted kite specialists, we have always stood by:

  • High-quality, easy-to-fly kite products
  • Bringing the joy of kite flying to every family
  • Preserving and promoting kite culture
  • Professional buying advice and service

The Mak Family

Three generations of the Mak family have been in the kite business in Hong Kong. Brothers Benny Mak and Jack Mak carry on their mother Leung Tai's founding spirit — blending traditional kite culture with modern needs to offer Hong Kong residents a wide variety of kites.

We believe kite flying is more than just an outdoor activity. It's a beautiful experience of family bonding, stress relief, and connecting with nature.

The Mak family — a family-run business dedicated to sharing the joy of kite flying under Hong Kong's open skies.

Our Products

CS Kites carries over 50 kites across a wide range of styles and skill levels:

  • Children's Kites: Ideal for ages 4–10, easy to fly in light wind, simple to assemble
  • Eagle / Golden Eagle Kites: Traditional Chinese design with stunning visual impact
  • Delta Kites: Best choice for beginners, stable and easy to fly
  • Animal Kites: Dinosaurs, butterflies, sea turtles, goldfish and more
  • Stunt / Aircraft Kites: For more experienced flyers
  • Large Kites: Visually spectacular, great for outdoor events
  • DIY Kite Materials: Fun parent-child crafting activity

Every product is carefully selected for quality and flight stability. We also provide expert buying advice to help you find the perfect kite.
